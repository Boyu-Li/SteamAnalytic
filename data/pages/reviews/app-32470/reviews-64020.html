\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you ever played this when it first came out back in 2006, then you should totally pick it up again. It's classic Star Wars goodness at When Star Wars was really booming. It is not a simple game to jump into if you have no experience with Real Time Strategy games. Heck, I had forgotten or never realized how hard an RTS could be until I bought this. If you like Star Wars, especially Extended Universe Star Wars, then you will enjoy this whether you have had experience with RTS games before or not. Locales, sound design, and all manner of heroes and villains to use from multiple eras. The modding community really gives it some new life, too, as there are some excellent mods out there that expands the game to include more classic, EU, and Disney canons. Plus the fact that it can be played at your own pace adds to its value. Really a top-notch RTS any Star Wars fan can appreciate.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have played this game for a while and I still play it but there is a thing I really REALLY hate.

Ground battles

Ground battles are boring. They have no tactics except chase your enemy. Even if you destroy their base, there are bound to be some light speeders or infantry left which is annoying because then you have to look for them around the whole map. This issue isn't that bad because you can just auto resolve and you can win if there are only a small amount of speeders or troops left, But they feel underwhelming. Playing as rebels and are fighting the empire on its last planet? Find and destroy the enemy base then auto resolve.

However other than that, I totally recommend this game. This is one of the best strategy games I've ever played especially because of the awesome mods for it.
